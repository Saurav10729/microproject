What is Get a Recipe ?

Introduction

 Get A Recipe is an web application that uses web scraping to give you desired recipes at just 1 click. it searches over multiple websites to find multiple recipes for you to cook from, given the limited number of ingredient you have.

For this web platform we scraped the recipes from 3 different sites.
Objectives
To provide an easy interface through which they can find recipes based on ingriedents they have.

To save time of user spent in searching for recipes in multiple sites.
Expected Outcome

 We plan to provide an all in one place to look for recipes easily.
we will try to remove hassle of going through multiple sites just to not get recipes at all
